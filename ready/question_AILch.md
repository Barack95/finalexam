A command that changes the timestamp of ``file`` to match yesterday's date.

* ``touch -t 'yesterday' file``
* ``touch -t '-1 day' file``
* ``touch -d 'keshe' file``
+ ``touch -d 'yesterday' file``
+ ``touch -d '-1 day' file``
* ``timestamp 'yestoday' file``
* ``time -d '-1 day' file``
* ``kiss -d '-1 day' file``
